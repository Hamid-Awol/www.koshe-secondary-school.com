About Our School

Founded in 1988, Koshe Secondary School has been providing quality education to students in Mareko Special Woreda for over three decades. Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in higher education and life.

Located in the heart of Central Ethiopia, we offer a comprehensive curriculum with modern facilities and experienced teaching staff dedicated to student success.
